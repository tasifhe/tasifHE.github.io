PROJECT OVERVIEW

Project Crack Platoon (PCP) is a tactical military FPS shooter that combines realistic combat mechanics with strategic infiltration gameplay and deep progression systems. Players lead elite operatives through high-stakes missions, utilizing advanced tactics, stealth, and cutting-edge military technology. Developed under Ghost Interactive, PCP features semi open world environments for dynamic mission approaches.

TACTICAL COMBAT

Engage in realistic firefights with advanced gunplay, ballistics, and cover systems. Every shot and movement counts in high-stakes scenarios.

STRATEGIC INFILTRATION

Utilize stealth, gadgets, and squad tactics to infiltrate enemy territory. Plan your approach and adapt to evolving threats.

DEEP PROGRESSION

Unlock new skills, gear, and operatives through a robust progression system. Customize your squad and loadouts for every mission.

SEMI OPEN WORLD

Explore expansive, semi open world maps that offer multiple paths and dynamic mission objectives.
